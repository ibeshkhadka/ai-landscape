Latest Models and Products

Qwen 3.7 Max LLM

Text-only flagship released May 2026. Intelligence Index v4.0 score of 56.6 — #1 Chinese model, 5th overall globally. 1M token context window, 50.8% on Terminal-Bench Hard, lowest hallucination rate among frontier models at 22.9%. API-only.

Qwen 3.7 Plus VLM

Multimodal variant with vision understanding. 1M context window. Designed for document, image, and video understanding. API-only.

Qwen 3.5 Family LLM

Open-weight family under Apache 2.0 (Feb–Mar 2026). Spans 0.8B to 397B-A17B (MoE flagship). Hybrid Gated DeltaNet + softmax attention architecture with 256K native context. 397B flagship hits 88.4 GPQA Diamond, 91.3 AIME 2026. Self-hostable on commodity GPUs.

Qwen 3.5 Omni AUDIO

Native multimodal model for text, audio, and video with real-time interaction capabilities. Released March 2026.

QwQ LLM

Dedicated reasoning model with strong math, code, and logic benchmarks. Part of the Qwen ecosystem.

Recent Activity and Context

Alibaba is the most vertically integrated AI company in China — spanning proprietary T-Head GPUs, Alibaba Cloud infrastructure, the full Qwen model stack, and consumer apps (Qwen App surpassed 300M MAU). Qwen is the most downloaded open-source model family on Hugging Face, exceeding 1B cumulative downloads as of January 2026. The 2026 releases show a clear pattern: frontier closed models (3.7 Max/Plus) funded by a broad open-weight family (3.5) under permissive licensing, all connected to Alibaba Cloud's API ecosystem.
