Latest Models and Products

Grok 4.3 LLM

Current flagship chat model (April 2026). 1M token context window. Native video input, in-chat document/slide/spreadsheet generation. Strong agentic tool calling with minimal hallucinations. $1.25/$2.50 per M tokens.

Grok 4.20 LLM

Multi-agent architecture with four specialized agents debating internally before returning a single answer. Reasoning and non-reasoning variants. Up to 2M context. Built-in orchestration at ~1.5-2.5x single-model cost. Released February 2026.

Grok Build 0.1 LLM

Purpose-built coding model for agentic workflows (May 2026). Successor to grok-code-fast-1. 256K context. Optimized for multi-step actions over single-turn Q&A.

Grok Voice & Imagine Video 1.5 VOICE

Grok Voice launched June 4, 2026 — conversational spoken interaction on mobile. Grok Imagine Video 1.5 Preview (#1 on Image-to-Video Arena) generates 720p clips up to 15s with native audio.
