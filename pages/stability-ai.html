Latest Models and Products

Stable Diffusion 4 (Announced) IMAGE

Next-generation image generation family announced Apr 2026 with upgraded DiT architecture. SD4 Ultra targets native 4096x4096 resolution, improved photorealism, and dedicated text rendering. Two tiers: SD4 Base (community) and SD4 Ultra (professional).

Stable Diffusion 3.5 IMAGE

Current open image generation family with Large (8.1B params), Large Turbo, and Medium variants. Strong prompt adherence and diverse style support.

Stable Audio 3.0 AUDIO

Latest audio model family released May 2026 capable of generating professional-grade music of over 6 minutes. Available in Small and Medium variants.

Stable Video Diffusion VIDEO

Open video generation model for text-to-video, image-to-video, and frame interpolation. Supports multi-view synthesis and short-form video creation with temporal coherence.

Stable Fast 3D 3D

Single-image to 3D asset generation model. Produces textured 3D meshes from a single input image in seconds. Used for game asset creation, e-commerce, and virtual worlds.

Recent Activity and Context

Stability AI announced Stable Diffusion 4 in April 2026, marking the first major architectural shift since SDXL — moving from U-Net to DiT. The portfolio now spans image (SD4, SD3.5), video (Stable Video Diffusion), 3D (Stable Fast 3D), and audio (Stable Audio 3.0), making Stability the broadest open generative media platform.
