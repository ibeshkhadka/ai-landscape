Latest Models and Products

Mistral Large 3 LLM

Flagship 675B MoE model (41B active) released Dec 2025 under Apache 2.0. Multimodal, multilingual, top-ranked open-weight model. Trained on 3,000 NVIDIA H200 GPUs. Strong reasoning, coding, and vision performance.

Ministral 3 (14B / 8B / 3B) LLM

Family of dense, state-of-the-art small models for low-latency edge deployment. Best performance-to-cost ratio in their class. Ideal for laptops, drones, and resource-constrained environments.

Codestral / Devstral 2 LLM

Code-specialized models for completion, refactoring, and multi-language generation. Devstral 2 extends to development workflows, tool integration, and DevOps.

Recent Activity and Context

Mistral's Mistral Large 3 is one of the top open-weight models globally, competing with Llama 4 and DeepSeek. The company now offers a complete stack from frontier (Large 3) to edge (Ministral 3) to specialized (Codestral), all under permissive Apache 2.0 licensing.
